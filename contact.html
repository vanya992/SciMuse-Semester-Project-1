Entrance is free for all!

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour.

You do not need to book it, writing yourself on the list is done by the entrance, includes a handy printed guide of the museum and costs 70nok.

If you want to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Food and drink

There is cafe attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you have left the museum.
